About Me

Hello my name is Neil,

I'm at Bellevue College to get a BAS IST in Application Dev. I have previously taken a Web Development course through an online college but since it was over 5 years ago, Bellevue College required I take the course again. In Programming, I have some knowledge in Python, I took a Professional beginner course through UW during the Spring. I'm hoping to continue learning Python as I found the language much easier to learn/use than Java. I've also worked extensively in Networking/TCPIP in previous careers. I'm hoping this class furthers my knowledge in HTML and CSS.

I enjoy traveling and hiking with my wife. I'm an avid reader, especially historical novels. I love playing baseball. I'm in an adult's league, the PSSBL, that plays 22 games throughout the summer. During the fall and winter, when the temperatures are lower, I love to brew my own beer at home. I currently have a cider in the fermentor. This is my first attempt at cider. Once a year the local brew supply store gets fresh pressed apples. I was fortunate to get my order in early and pick up 5 gallons The apples are not you're grocery variety kind, they're bittersweet apples that are perfect for dry ciders. Cheers!
