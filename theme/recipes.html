Berliner Weisse

  • 1 pound 5357 White Wheat Malt
  • 1 pound 1866 Red Pilsner Malt
  • 1 pound 1850 Pilsner Malt
  • 3 pounds CBW Briess Bavarian Wheat Dried Malt Extract (DME)
  • 3 pounds CBW Briess Golden Light DME
  • Wyeast 3944 Belgian Wit
  • 1 Hallertau package of Hop pellets-2.5%A

Steps and Observations

            3/7/17
            Used a grian bag and mashed for 45 minutes.
            Put in one DME bag in early before the sparge. After sparge threw in the other.
            Put hops in for 15 minutes. Only boiled for 15 minutes. 
            Cooled and added the yeast.
            OG reads 1.060 but that was measured from the bottom of the brew, sediment likely tainted reading
            3/12/17-Added the Wyeast 3944, Lactobacillus
            First 3 days of fermentation were very active, settled after that.

Sour Wheat Beer

  • 1 pound CBW Golden Light DME
  • 3 pounds CBW Golden Light DME
  • 3 pounds CBW Bavarian Wheat
  • WLP 672 Lactobacillus Brevis
  • Wyeast 3056 Bavarian Wheat
  • 1 package Halleratu pellet hops

Steps and Observations

             12/10/17, Ptiched Lactobacillus at 68 degrees, should have pitched at 80.
             12-15, Finally some fermentation, slow and steady. Light layer of Kraussen.
             12-18, Pitched 3056 Yeast at 22:25 hours.
             12-19, Activity in air valve, 6:15 hours.
             12-20, One air bubble every 50 seconds, light layer of Kraussen.
             12-20, 18:00 hours, air bubble every 35 seconds.
             12-21, air bubble every 20 seconds.
             12-22, air bubble every 20 seconds.

German Roggenbier

  • 5lbs Munich Malt
  • 5lbs Rye Malt
  • 2lbs Flaked Rye
  • 1lbs Crystal Rye
  • 1oz package of Tennnang Hops
  • 1ox Carafa Specail II

Steps and Observations

            1-29-18, First Brew Attempt with new Brewer's Edge (BE) All-Grain Brewing System.
            Placed 3.9 Gallons of strike water in BE, used hot sink tap water. Resulted in temp of 109 degrees at 1755.
            1805, temp is at 129 degrees, 1810 at 143.
            20:03, difficulty keeping temps at steady 150 temp. Changed power to 1000W instead of 1600W.
            Experiencing difficulty sparging and draining malts. Had to move grain around and unblock screen.
            Used too much Rye in the recipe, it is a mess and creating draining issues. 
            This recipe did not turn out well, had to dump out.
